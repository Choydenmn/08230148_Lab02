About Me

Hi, I am Kinzang Choden, hailing from Banjar under Mongar Dzongkhag. I am a girl filled with endless hopes and dreams. I once longed to become a geography teacher, but fate took a different turn. Yet, I am not disappointed with where I am today, because I am still on the path of fulfilling the dream I have cherished since childhood to become a teacher. Currently, I am pursuing my journey toward becoming an aspiring ICT teacher, with the vision of inspiring and nurturing young minds. I believe that teaching is not just about imparting knowledge, but also about empowering students to think critically, explore creatively, and grow confidently. I enjoy learning new things, embracing challenges, and discovering opportunities that broaden my perspectives. With passion, perseverance, and dedication, I hope to contribute meaningfully to education and create a positive impact in the lives of future generations.
